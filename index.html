MotivArt

Create and place virtual frames
with motivational quotes or personal messages



MotivArt is an app designed for Vision OS that allows you to create and display motivational quotes and personal messages or reminders in the form of virtual frames. With a wide selection of inspiring phrases or the option to write your own messages, you can customize and place these frames anywhere in your physical space. Whether at home, in the office, or any environment, MotivArt helps you stay motivated, positive, and focused on your goals. Transform your reality with words that inspire and remind you of the importance of moving forward.

  • 3D Spatial Experience: Place your motivational messages in your 3D space. Position them anywhere in your environment to accompany you throughout the day.
  • Quote Frames: Design your frames with a curated selection of inspiring quotes or write your own to keep motivation and positivity alive in your life.
  • Frame Styles: Choose from a variety of background colors and fonts to match your aesthetic and mood.
